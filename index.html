The purpose of this tool is to help you narrow your search along your information journey; this is not a diagnostic tool.Our Symptom checker suggests potential medical conditions by comparing your symptoms with our database. This tool does not incorporate all the personal, health and demographic factors related to you, individually, that would allow a definitive cause or causes to be suggested.The most reliable way to determine the cause of your symptom, and what to do, is to visit your health-care provider.
